Meet Maggie

Web designer & developer

I am a day dreamer by nature who loves to be inspired by everything around me. I find a lot of inspiration from different artist like Banksy and Walt Disney. I love experimenting with CSS and animation to create unique work. I have a desire to learn everything I can so I spend a lot of my off time watching tutorials as well as keeping up on all of the up and coming trends.

  • Web Design

    I love taking a concept or idea and turn it into a full layout using the Adobe suite. Working with CSS, SASS and JavaScript to bring a site to life is a really amazing process. I am always working to advance my skills in this area.

  • Web Development

    Over the course of my schooling I have been able to build skills working with different frameworks such as: PHP, MySQL, ASP.NET, MEAN Stack as well as working with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have also had to the chance to work with WordPress building child themes and working with Ecommerce.

  • Interactive Media

    When working from design to development you need to pay attention to UX/UI design to insure your site is going to be usable for everyone. This is where you can also add in animation to add to the users experience and really add to the the over all brand and feel of site.
